Development and Production Forms

LapysJS renders scripting files that are tailored for both beginner-level developers to have a better time with JavaScript as a language and pro-level programmers to enjoy a more easing and flexible feel to the language.
LapysJS gives you two forms of its JavaScript once downloaded: The compressed and minified form for use on production sites facing the web. You can find it in the src folder as the lapys.min.js file. and the default and manually coded form for use in development sites. You can also find it in the src folder as lapys.js.

Strict JavaScript Rules

It is suggested at the user’s will to modify the content of the file to nullify or enhance any strict rules the library declares.

LapysCSS

The lapys.js and lapys.min.js files work in tandem with the lapys.css and lapys.min.css files for some of its plug-ins and not independently because all its plug-ins are based off visual styles, dynamism & animation on a web page.

Design is not Functionality

The lapys.css and lapys.min.css files do not need the lapys.js and lapys.min.js files because they are functionally independent and focus and design and style only.

LapysJS makes modifications to the regular everyday list of items known as Arrays.
Arrays which are useful for non-singular data manipulation have always been needed for ordering the control flow of our scripting language, sorting a group of objects in an OOP language or any other idea our developers can think of. But most of the time, Arrays aren’t necessarily easy to master, you will have to learn how to manipulate data to and from an array and methods like push and pop can not be justified as substitutes to methods that would&rquo;ve been called addNewElement and removeOldElement.
LapysJS allows developers to work with arrays more neatly whilst holding the hands of new and diving coders and allowing a simpler, more flexible alternative to at first arduous tasks for steady programmers.
